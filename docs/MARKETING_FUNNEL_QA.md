## Question 2: What's the best marketing channel for this service?

### Answer: **LinkedIn Marketing (Primary Channel)**

### Why LinkedIn?

1. **Perfect Audience Match**
   - VCs, biotech executives, pharma decision-makers all use LinkedIn
   - Professional context = higher purchase intent
   - Easy targeting by industry, job title, company size

2. **High ROI**
   - B2B LinkedIn campaigns: 2-5% conversion rates
   - Cost per lead: $50-150 (vs $200+ for Google Ads)
   - Lower competition than Google

3. **Multiple Tactics**
   - LinkedIn Ads: Sponsored content, message ads
   - LinkedIn Content: Organic posts, thought leadership
   - LinkedIn Outreach: Direct messaging

### Recommended Channel Mix

**Primary**: LinkedIn Ads + Content ($500-1500/month)
- Sponsored content to landing page
- Thought leadership posts
- Direct message ads

**Secondary**: Email Nurturing ($0-50/month)
- Automated sequences for captured leads
- Newsletter for ongoing engagement

**Tertiary**: SEO/Content Marketing ($0-500/month)
- Blog posts for organic traffic
- Case studies, whitepapers

**Total Budget**: $500-2000/month to start
